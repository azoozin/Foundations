Miso Chicken Soup

Miso chicken noodle soup is a comforting dish featuring tender chicken, chewy noodles, and vibrant vegetables simmered in a savory miso broth.
The umami-rich miso paste enhances the flavors, while fresh herbs and spices add depth.
This nourishing soup is perfect for warming the soul on chilly days.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 8.5 ounces frozen udon noodle
  • 4 ounces rotisserie chicken, shredded
  • 1 tablespoon white miso paste
  • everything bagel seasoning, to taste

Directions

  1. In a saucepan over medium high heat, heat water to boiling. Add udon noodles.
    Cook until noodles are softened, 4 to 5 minutes.
  2. Add rotisserie chicken. Turn heat to low.
    Remove 2 to 3 tablespoons water, and stir miso paste into water until smooth. Mix diluted miso paste back into noodles.
    Cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
  3. Ladle soup into bowls and garnish with everything bagel seasoning.
